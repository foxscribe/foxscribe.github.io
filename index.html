About me

Hi! My name is Iaroslav, I'm 18. I'm a software developer, amateur artist, musician and writer. In my spare time I also explore video editing and motion graphics. I really like old devices and software and often use them as a working tool.

I am currently studying software engineering at university. Also, I finished music school in piano class in 2022 and graduated from a lyceum with physics and maths major in 2024. In addition to all that, I am self-educating: I am developing my skills in writing stories and composing music, learning Objective-C and Pascal.

In the past, I have used various UNIX-like systems extensively, including GNU/Linux and BSD distributions. My favourite is Fedora, but I also love Debian and Red Hat Enterprise Linux. At this moment I actively using Mac OS X.

I don't play games very often, but I can name some of my favourites: Minecraft, OpenTTD and TIS-100. Maybe someday I will create my own game.

Goals

One of my main goals in life is to develop simple software that solves complex problems. Creativity is no less important to me - I want to get really good at drawing, composing music and writing stories. I'd also like to spread knowledge to people, so I'm thinking about creating a video blog. Last but not least, I would like to learn how to create really cool things with video editing and motion graphics.
